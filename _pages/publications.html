My research spans computer vision, multimodal perception, semantic segmentation, and robotic place recognition. An asterisk-style role note is written explicitly for papers with shared or student authorship.

Journal Articles

PHMRNet: Persistent Homology based Mamba-RWKV Network for LiDAR Place Recognition

Dejing Zhou et al.
IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters, 2026. First author. Transferred to ICRA 2027.
DOI: 10.1109/LRA.2026.3683589

Combines persistent-homology descriptors with Mamba/RWKV spatiotemporal modeling for robust LiDAR place recognition under occlusion, viewpoint rotation, and dynamic interference.

MSCF-LUNet: A Lightweight Three-Stage Pine Wilt Disease Segmentation Model with Multi-Scale Context Fusion Mechanism

Dejing Zhou, Junxian Chen, et al.
Frontiers in Plant Science, 2025/2026. Co-first and first-listed author.
DOI: 10.3389/fpls.2025.1727626

Introduces attention-guided multi-scale patch embedding and contextual fusion for lightweight segmentation of small, weak-texture, and ambiguous disease regions in low-altitude remote-sensing imagery.

Improved Model for Low Altitude Detection of Trees Infected by Pests and Diseases Using Agricultural Drones Based on DCA-YOLO

Mentor first author; Dejing Zhou as first student author.

Conference Papers

SMBF: Saliency-Guided Multi-modal Feature Bridging and Bidirectional Fusion for RGB-T Semantic Segmentation

Dejing Zhou et al.
IEEE International Conference on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics (SMC). Accepted; first author.

Uses saliency context modeling, prototype-level semantic bridging, and bidirectional cross-modal attention to improve RGB-T segmentation under day-night variations and complex scenes.

Manuscripts

DGAOT: Dynamic Graph Attention with Optimal Transport based Re-ranking for Mobile Robot Visual Place Recognition

Dejing Zhou et al.
Under review at IEEE Internet of Things Journal. Co-first author.

Develops differentiable dynamic graph aggregation, optimal-transport score redistribution, and coarse-to-fine average-precision optimization for real-time visual place-recognition re-ranking.
